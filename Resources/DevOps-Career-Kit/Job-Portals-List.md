# Where to Apply? (Job Portals)

Don't rely solely on LinkedIn.

## 1. Startup / Tech Focused (Best for Interns)
- **Wellfound (formerly AngelList)**: [wellfound.com](https://wellfound.com/)
  - *Why*: Startups often value passion over years of experience. High response rate.
- **Y Combinator Jobs**: [ycombinator.com/jobs](https://www.ycombinator.com/jobs)
  - *Why*: The best startups in the world.

## 2. Remote Focused
- **We Work Remotely**: [weworkremotely.com](https://weworkremotely.com/)
- **RemoteOK**: [remoteok.com](https://remoteok.com/)

## 3. The Giants
- **LinkedIn Jobs**: (Use filters: "Entry Level" + "Posted in last 24 hours").
- **Indeed**: Generic, but huge volume.

## 4. Specific to DevOps
- **DevOpsGlance**: [devopsglance.com](https://devopsglance.com/)

---
**Strategy**: Apply to 5 jobs a day, every day. It's a numbers game.
