# Cold Email Templates for DevOps

Personalize anything in `[Brackets]`!

## 1. Asking for an Internship (To Founders/CTOs)
**Subject**: Quick question from a young DevOps enthusiast

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Company Name] and love how you are [mention a specific feature/achievement].

I am a final year student who has spent the last 6 months mastering Docker, Kubernetes, and AWS (I recently deployed a serverless resume on S3 + CloudFront).

I know you aren't actively hiring interns, but I am looking for a place to learn and contribute. I am happy to work for free/stipend to prove my value.

Here is my portfolio: [Link]

Best,
[Your Name]

## 2. Connecting with a Senior Engineer (Mentorship)
**Subject**: Your post about [Topic] inspired me

Hi [Name],

I saw your recent post about [Topic/Terraform/Kubernetes] on LinkedIn. It cleared up a lot of confusion I had about [Specific Detail].

I am aspiring to become a DevOps Engineer. I wouldn't dare ask for a job, but I’d love to ask one question:

*"If you were starting today, would you focus more on Python scripting or Go?"*

Thanks for your content!

Best,
[Your Name]

## 3. The "Follow Up" (Persistence wins)
**Subject**: Re: Quick question

Hi [Name],

I know you are incredibly busy building [Product], so no need to reply to this email.

Just wanted to float this to the top of your inbox in case it got buried. I’m still very keen to help [Company] optimize their CI/CD pipelines.

Best,
[Your Name]
