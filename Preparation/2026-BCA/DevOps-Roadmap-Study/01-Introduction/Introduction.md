# DevOps Roadmap Introduction

Welcome to your DevOps learning journey! This guide is based on the **DevOps Full Roadmap** (2024) by Code with Mosh.

## Goal
The goal of this roadmap is to provide a clear and structured path for beginners and experienced individuals to become proficient DevOps Engineers.

## Target Audience
- **Beginners**: Those looking to land their first DevOps job.
- **Experienced Individuals**: Those looking to level up their skills or fill gaps in their knowledge.

## Estimated Timeline
Becoming a proficient DevOps engineer typically takes **10-14 months**, assuming **3 to 5 hours** of dedicated study every day.

## Learning Phases
- **Beginner**: Linux, Networking, Git, Python.
- **Intermediate**: AWS, Docker, Jenkins, Kubernetes, Nginx.
- **Advanced**: Ansible, Terraform, Prometheus/Grafana/ELK.
