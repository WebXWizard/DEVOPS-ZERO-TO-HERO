# LinkedIn Growth Mastery

LinkedIn is your free ticket to a job. Recruiters live here.

## 1. Profile Optimization
- **Banner**: Not a generic landscape. Use Canva to make a banner that says "Aspiring Cloud/DevOps Engineer".
- **Headline**: Don't just put "Student". Use:
  > Aspiring DevOps Engineer | AWS | Docker | Kubernetes | Learning continuously.
- **Featured Section**: Pin your "Cloud Resume Challenge" post here.

## 2. The 10-Day Posting Challenge
Don't know what to post? Here is a schedule.

- **Day 1**: "I am starting my DevOps journey today. Here is my roadmap." (Tag 3 friends).
- **Day 2**: "Today I learned Linux permissions. `chmod 777` is dangerous! Here is why..."
- **Day 3**: "Git is harder than it looks. Merge vs Rebase?"
- **Day 4**: "Just deployed my first Docker container. Magic."
- **Day 5**: (Share a picture of your setup/screen). "Late night coding session."
- **Day 6**: "Kubernetes architecture explained simply." (Use an analogy).
- **Day 7**: "Why I chose AWS over Azure."
- **Day 8**: "Automating tasks with Python."
- **Day 9**: "My biggest failure so far (and what I fixed)."
- **Day 10**: "Completed my first project! Here is the link."

## 3. How to Connect
Don't just click "Connect". Add a note:
> "Hi [Name], I noticed you work at [Company] as a DevOps Engineer. I am just starting out and would love to follow your journey. No sales, just learning!"
