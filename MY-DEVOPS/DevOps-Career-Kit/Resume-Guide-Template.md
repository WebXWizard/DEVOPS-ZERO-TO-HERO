1.  **Length**: 1 Page. Period.
2.  **Format**: Clean, PDF. No photos, no fancy graphics.
3.  **Metrics**: Use specific numbers!

## The STAR Method
When writing bullets, use **STAR**:
- **S**ituation (The problem)
- **T**ask (What you had to do)
- **A**ction (What tool did you use?)
- **R**esult (The outcome/number).

*Bad*: "Used Docker."
*Good (STAR)*: "Containerized legacy applications using **Docker** (Action) to eliminate 'works on my machine' issues (Situation), reducing onboarding time by 50% (Result)."
